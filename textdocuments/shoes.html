Shoes

Jordans

Nike's Jordans are one of the most well-known shoe brands in the US. Their great fit and grip on the court is what makes them notorious. Additionally, the shoe brand was made after the G.O.A.T. himself, Michael Jordan which makes the shoe ever more special.

Low-High Tops

The Nike Low-High tops are one of the most popular lifestyle shoes on the market, and rightfully so. They are fashionable, classy, and comfortable. Always in the right colors and always in plentiful options, keeping the game fresh.

Cleats

Nike cleats are made with the finest of leathers and rubbers and built for specific needs. Some are lightweight for faster speed and agility, while others are more pointy and firm for strength and grip. It all comes down to who you are and where you want to go.

Origin of Jordans

Jordans were first introduced in 1984 as a gift for the all time great, Michael Jordan. They were one of the first branded shoes made specifially after a player, and changed the basketball shoe scene forever.

Origin of Low-High Tops

Low-High Tops were invented in 1949, providing a new look on shoe fashion. Initially made by Converse, the alternative look caught people by the eye. Soon enough, these shoes started selling left and right, changing the fashion industry forever.

Origin of Cleats

Developed in the 1890s by Reebok, cleats were one of the most earliest and game-changing shoe product to ever be released. The provided a new and stronger grip while increasing speed and agility. Now they are used across almost every sport applicable and have been modified to be even better than ever.
