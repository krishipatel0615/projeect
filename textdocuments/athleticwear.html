Athletic Wear

Hoodies and Sweatshirts

Nike's athletic wear covers all aspects of modern day sports. Let's start off with the hoodies and sweatshirts. Nike's hoodies and sweatshirts are mad specifically for the outdoors; whether it's raining or snowing, the material is built for it. They are also made to be as light as possible, allowing for the most unrestrained movement allowed while wearing the hoodie.

Shorts and Joggers

The shorts and joggers made by Nike are the most premium quality you will find in the country. They are made up 75% recycled polyster fabric, making them not only lightweight but also good for the environment. With the lightness of the material also comes the flexibility. Not only will you get great range of movement with the shorts, you will also be able to do the same in the joggers, which are made for athletic tasks that are performed in cold environments.

Jackets, Training Exclusives, and Accessories

Jackets made at Nike are made exclusively for difficult weather and are tested to ensure that they can withstand what is thrown at them. Some training exclusives we provide are sturdy training cones, ladders, poles, and essential equipment for each sport, all which are made to the highest standard. Other accessories include fitted and regulars hats, water bottles, and backpacks.

Origin of Hoodies and Sweatshirts

Hoodies and Sweatshirts first became a thing in 1919. They were introduced by Knickerbocker Knitting Company, now known as Champion, who brought the idea to the public. Now, it is one of the most used clothingwear during the colder seasons.

Origin of Shorts and Joggers

Shorts and Joggers have been around since the beginning of the human era. They have been quite quintessential in a person's daily life as they are not only functional but fashionable too. They are an extremely well selling piece of clothing today, and will continue to be so for a very long time.

Origin of Jackets and Accessories

Jackets have also been around since the start of the human era, and have been used since the very first winter. They are one of the most useful pieces of clothing in the cold seasons, as they keep the body warm and fucntional. Accessories like bottles and bags date back to many thousand years BCE, and have been very effective in helping humans in their daily lives.
